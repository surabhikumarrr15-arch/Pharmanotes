❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is pharmacovigilance? ▼
Pharmacovigilance is the science of monitoring drug safety — detecting, assessing and preventing adverse drug reactions.
What is regulatory affairs? ▼
Regulatory affairs professionals handle drug approvals and submissions to authorities like CDSCO, FDA & EMA.
How to choose a project topic for Sem 8? ▼
Choose a topic you are passionate about — formulation development, drug analysis or pharmacological studies are popular choices.
After B Pharma what can I do? ▼
You can pursue M Pharma, MBA Pharma, GPAT, government jobs (Drug Inspector), hospital pharmacy or pharmaceutical industry jobs.
