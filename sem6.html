❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is pharmaceutical biotechnology? ▼
It covers how biological systems and organisms are used to produce medicines like insulin, vaccines and antibodies.
Is quality assurance important for pharma jobs? ▼
Absolutely! QA/QC is one of the most in-demand fields in pharma industry. Sem 6 gives you strong foundation.
What is GMP? ▼
Good Manufacturing Practice — a system of regulations ensuring medicines are consistently produced and controlled to quality standards.
Are Sem 6 notes helpful for GPAT? ▼
Yes, biotechnology and quality assurance are important GPAT topics covered in these notes.
